Welcome to Butchery Meat Shop

Our History

In 1998, Thompson's Steakhouse was founded by John Thompson, a man whose passion for quality meat was instilled in him from a young age. As the son of a skilled butcher, John grew up surrounded by the sights and smells of his father's shop, learning the art of selecting and preparing the finest cuts. This early education fueled his dream of creating a steakhouse where he could share his love for exceptional meat with the community. With a vision to offer an unparalleled dining experience, John opened Thompson's Steakhouse, combining traditional butchery expertise with a welcoming, elegant atmosphere.

Since its opening, Thompson's Steakhouse has become a cornerstone of the local dining scene. John's unwavering commitment to quality and his innovative approach to classic steakhouse cuisine quickly garnered a devoted following. Each steak is meticulously sourced, aged to perfection, and expertly grilled, reflecting John's dedication to his craft. Over the years, the restaurant has expanded its menu to include a variety of gourmet sides and a curated wine list, enhancing the overall dining experience. Today, Thompson's Steakhouse stands as a testament to John Thompson's vision, offering a timeless celebration of fine dining and his family's rich butcher heritage.
